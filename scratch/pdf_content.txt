BLOCKER 04

         initial-scale=1">

T·1
VIDEO STREAMING SPECIALIZATION
File-on-CDN → HLS/DASH adaptive stream
Seen in MasterClass · Coursera
Stack Mux · Cloudflare Stream
Adaptive bitrate — zero buffering at any speed

Bandwidth ~70% cheaper than Blob at scale
T·2
SERVERLESS ASYNC QUEUES
Event-driven fanout with retries
Seen in ClassDojo · Duolingo
Stack Inngest · Upstash QStash · AWS SQS
Push, payment hooks, email — never fire inline
Auto-retry 5–10× on serverless cold-start drops
Master-app signup alert loss rate → 0%
T·3
NOSQL + RDBMS HYBRID
Right tool per workload
Keep Firebase for auth · realtime chat

"Exclusive territory within 5mi" — 100ms SQL, not a scan
"Top-converting dojos last 7d" — cheap aggregate
T·4

Decide at the edge, not the client

US LAUNCH BLOCKER
COPPA exposure
$50,120
p e r  c h i l d  ·  m a x  f i n e
The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act requires verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal data from users under 13. FTC
has issued nine-figure fines to YouTube and Epic Games for non-compliance.
WHAT WE COLLECT FROM MINORS TODAY
Student accounts — name, email, password
Video telemetry — device, IP, browser, watch time, completion rate,
pause/seek events
Interaction traces — click streams, menu access patterns
All three categories qualify as "personal information" under
COPPA §312.2.
FINE MATH AT SCALE
100 students · one violation each
$5.0M
1,000 students · one violation each
$50.1M
YouTube · 2019 settlement
$170M
Strict liability. Intent is not a defense.
